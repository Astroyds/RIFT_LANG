RIFT — more with less

Language pillars

Design decisions focus on readability, minimal surface area, and a systems-focused flow.

Minimal syntax

Distinct block and array delimiters reduce noise and keep structural cues visible in dense logic.

Reduced boilerplate

Pipeline operators and concise declarations keep intent on the line without scaffolding.

Composable primitives

Functions, pattern checks, and modules are built for composition instead of ceremony.

Readability-first design

RIFT favors explicit data flow, compact literals, and a consistent visual grammar.

Enterprise-ready foundations

Standard modules include HTTP, JSON, crypto, and database tooling with clear entry points.
